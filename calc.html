Calculus Simulator

How to interact:

Derivative: Drag the green point along the curve to see the tangent line (slope) change.

Integral: Drag the two red points on the x-axis to change the shaded area under the curve.

Input tip: Use plain notation: sin(x), cos(x), sqrt(x), log(x), abs(x) – no LaTeX backslashes needed.
(For nested expressions like abs(x^2-1), writing |x^2-1| also works.)
